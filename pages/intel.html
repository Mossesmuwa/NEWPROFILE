Threat Landscape

What We're Defending Against

Phishing
Fake emails or messages that trick people into giving up credentials. Responsible for over 90% of data breaches. Technically simple — psychologically sophisticated.
Learn more
Ransomware
Attackers encrypt your files and demand payment. Hospitals lose patient records. Schools lose years of data. Businesses pay millions. Recovery is never complete.
CISA guidance
Insider Threats
Employees with access who misuse it — intentionally or by accident. Harder to detect than external attacks because the attacker already has legitimate credentials.
CISA resource
Supply Chain Attacks
Compromise a trusted vendor to reach hundreds of targets at once. The SolarWinds attack reached 18,000 organisations through a single software update.
CISA advisory
Credential Theft
Stolen username and password combinations sold on dark web markets. Password reuse makes one breach into many. MFA is no longer optional — it's essential.
Check your email
Network Interception
Attackers capturing unencrypted traffic on shared networks. Exactly what I demonstrated in OP-004 — Telnet credentials visible in plain text to anyone sniffing the wire.
OWASP reference
